A guided tour of cutting-edge CSS you can use now

Welcome to a tour of cutting-edge CSS you can use now. These examples were inspired by this article: 5 CSS snippets every front-end developer should know in 2024. Front-end web developer Teri Shelton, in collaboration with designer Andy Krikawa, put together these examples.

At the May 19, 2024 Front-End Tech Community of Practice, we'll demonstrate how they work and share some ideas on use cases. There's so much to cover: from subgrid to container queries and :has() to color-scheme... and more! It's going to be like a guided tour of favorite sites: stop, look at the view, snap a photo (screenshot!), ask a question, hop back on the bus.
